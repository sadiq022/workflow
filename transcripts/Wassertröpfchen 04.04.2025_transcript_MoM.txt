# Minutes of Meeting: Wassertröpfchen Meeting

## Introduction

The Wassertröpfchen Meeting took place on April 4th. The meeting was attended by Herr Schmidt, Herr Bartke, Herr Schünemann, Herr Neuling, Herr Dölitz, Herr Lauschke, and Herr Bauer. The meeting covered various topics related to performance, process optimization, and future developments within the company.

## 1. Wassertropf Nummer 1

### Background
The first topic discussed was regarding Wassertropf Nummer 1, focusing on a special commitment made by Herr Bartke during the Searing post-processing.

### Key Points
- Herr Schmidt suggested that Herr Bartke receive a performance bonus for his special commitment.
- A discussion ensued about a target ring and the combination of two errors: the winding program not being released and the manufacturing protocol not being cleanly filled out.

### Observations
- The need to sharpen error awareness among team members.
- The importance of optimizing processes to prevent similar errors.

### Decisions
- It was decided that Herr Bartke would not receive a bonus since he himself had made an error.

### Actions
- Proposals for performance bonuses should be sent to Herr Bauer or the speaker.

## 2. Wassertropf Nummer zwei

### Background
The second topic concerned Wassertropf Nummer zwei, specifically addressing a problem with cleaning material in the coating process.

### Key Points
- Herr Schünemann utilized KI (Artificial Intelligence) to find a solution for the issue with the cleaning material in the coating process.

### Observations
- The potential for process optimization.
- The importance of quality awareness.

### Decisions
- Herr Schünemann was awarded a bonus of 100 Euros.

### Actions
- Herr Neuling is tasked with properly filing the data researched by Herr Schünemann.

## 3. Wassertropf Nummer drei

The meeting covered a range of topics from performance bonuses and process optimizations to future developments and performance measurements. Action items were assigned to various participants to follow up on the decisions made during the meeting.
